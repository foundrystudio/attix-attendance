About Attix

Attix is an AI-powered attendance calculator and attendance prediction tool designed for students.

It helps students calculate attendance percentages, predict safe bunk limits, recover attendance shortages, and stay ready for semester examinations.

The goal of Attix is to simplify attendance tracking and help students make informed academic decisions without manually calculating attendance records.

FAQ

Common questions

Is Attix official attendance software?

No. Attix is a student-focused attendance assistant that provides estimates based on the numbers you enter.

Can Attix predict my safe bunks?

Yes. Attix calculates how many remaining classes can be missed while staying at or above your required percentage.

Does Attix store my attendance data?

No. The calculator runs in your browser and is private by default.
