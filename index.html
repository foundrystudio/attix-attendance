About

About Attix

Attix is an AI-powered attendance calculator and attendance prediction tool designed for students.

Track attendance, calculate safe bunks, predict eligibility, and plan your semester attendance in seconds.

Attix is a fast attendance calculator for students who want to understand their attendance percentage, safe bunks, recovery required, and exam eligibility without spreadsheets or guesswork.

Inputs are calculated in your browser, so you can experiment with different attendance scenarios quickly and privately.

FAQ

Common questions

How is attendance calculated?

Attendance is calculated by dividing attended classes by classes held, then multiplying the result by 100.

How many classes can I bunk?

Yes. Attix calculates how many remaining classes can be missed while staying at or above your required percentage.

What is attendance recovery?

Attendance recovery is the number of upcoming classes you need to attend to reach your required attendance percentage.

Can Attix predict final attendance?

Yes. Attix forecasts your final attendance based on your current attendance and remaining classes.

Is Attix free to use?

Yes. Attix is free to use for everyday student attendance planning.

Does Attix store my attendance data?

No. The calculator runs in your browser and is private by default.

Is Attix official attendance software?

No. Attix is a student-focused attendance assistant that provides estimates based on the numbers you enter.
